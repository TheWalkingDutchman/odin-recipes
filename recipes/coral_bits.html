Coral Bits

Coral Bits are one of the many undersea delicacies served at the Krusty Krab.

Coral Bits are as simple as they sound: fresh coral shattered into bits is deep-fried at a temperature of 375o just long enough to obtain a golden-crisped crust and perfect crunch.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 pound of fresh coral, broken into bite-sized bits
  • 1 quart of frying oil with a high smoke point

STEPS:

  1. Pour oil in fryer and set heat to 375O
  2. Break coral into bite-sized pieces
  3. Gently immerse coral in heated oil and cook 6-10 minutes or until edges are crispy and golden
  4. Enjoy your Krusty Krab classic dish!
