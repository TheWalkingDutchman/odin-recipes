Kelp Fries

Kelp Fries are one of the many undersea delicacies served at the Krusty Krab.

Kelp Fries always begin with fresh sea potatoes, crinkle-cut in the perfect thickness for a crispy exterior and fluffy inside

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 pound of fresh sea potatoes, cut to shape with a sharp crinkle-cutter
  • 1 quart of frying oil with a high smoke point

STEPS:

  1. Pour oil in fryer and set heat to 375o
  2. Roughly peel exterior of potatoes, cut into fries with crinkle-cutter
  3. Gently immerse fries in heated oil and cook 6-10 minutes or until edges are crispy and golden
  4. Add bowtie to the best fry of the batch (optional)
  5. Enjoy your Krusty Krab classic dish!
